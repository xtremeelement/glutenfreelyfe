Gluten Free Biscuit Recipe

Amount Weight Item
5 Ounces White Rice Flour
1.5 Ounces Brown Rice Flour
1.5 Ounces Potato Starch
1 Ounces Tapioca Flour/Starch
1 Tablespoon Nonfat Milk Powder
4 Teaspoons Baking Powder
1.5 Teaspoons Psyllium Husk Powder
1 Teaspoons Sugar
1/2 Teaspoons Salt
1/4 Teaspoons Baking Soda
3/4 Stick Unsalted Butter, cut into 1/4 pieces
3/4 Cup Plain Whole Milk Yogurt
1 Large Egg
2 Tablespoon Oil (we used Grape Seed)
2 Tablespoon Lemon Juice

Instructions:

1. Mix all dry ingredients in bowl until fully incorporated.

2. Add sliced butter to dry flour mix.

3. In a separate bowl, whisk all wet ingredients together.

4. Slowly add the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix well.

5. Cover, and let batter rest for 20-30 minutes. (The longer the better)

6. Preheat oven to 450 degrees and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

7. Scoop batter into 6 equal portions onto baking sheet.

7. Bake for 14-16 minutes and turning sheet once half way through cooking process.

8. Once finished, remove from oven and let the biscuits cool for at least 5 minutes.

9. Enjoy!
